# Enable Anonymous Sign-ins

The error `Anonymous sign-ins are disabled` means this feature is turned off in your Supabase project settings. You must enable it manually.

**Steps:**
1. Go to your **Supabase Dashboard**.
2. Click on **Authentication** (the lock icon in the sidebar).
3. Click on **Providers** under the Configuration section.
4. Locate **Anonymous** in the list of providers.
5. Click to expand it and toggle **Enable Anonymous Sign-ins** to **ON**.
6. Click **Save**.

After saving, reload your application. The error should disappear.
